My Works

Dark Quiet Death

Dark Quiet Death is a Godot 4 survival-collection game written in GDScript with GDShader-powered lighting.

You explore procedurally generated dungeons, recharge your torch at scattered ports, and occasionally earn memory shards that drive the final confrontation.

Hand-drawn sprites, dynamic shadows and a minimalist soundscape create a tense atmosphere where every step of feels like a gamble.

Nerdifier

Nerdifier is a single-page app that turns productivity into a game.

It combines to-do lists, Pomodoro-style timers, in-app notes, scheduling, progress stats and leaderboards with calming background music.

A “lock-in” reward loop and timed boosts mimic corporate incentives to keep you hooked on leveling up.

Built from scratch with custom file routing and dynamic module loading for an instant and distraction-free learning experience.
